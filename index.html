About the theme:

The theme of this year’s convention is “Rise Above the Limits”

Whining about it rarely levels the playing field, but learning to rise above it is the ultimate reward.However, you can’t just dip your foot in the ocean of the unknown. You have to dive in, head first. It’s mind blowing what can happen when you choose to not give up.
You have the power to turn the impossible into the possible.

Journey of the Delegate:

When you become a delegate of the 18th Annual ISTE State Student Convention,you sign up for 2 Days of enhancing & engaging sessions, a multitude of events,an ultimate platform for networking and much more. Benefits
• ISTE issued Certificate of attendance of a Statewide Convention
• Meeting up with the best experts in the field
• Attending unlimited talks & session to gather insight. • Competitions with amazing prizes • An opportunity to test your limits
Where? Government Engineering College, Barton Hill was established by the Government of Kerala in the academic year 1999-2000. Within a short span of time since inception, the college has achieved notable academic excellence. The college is affiliated to APJ Abdul kalam Kerala Technological University. It is under the administrative control of the Director of Technical Education, Government of Kerala. We thrive to impart Engineering knowledge and skills, to mould professionals with integrity and to nurture the spirit of innovation, creativity and sustainability to address needs of society.
